# CHOps Pro

Everything documented in these guides is part of the free, open-source community edition of CHOps, licensed under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 (AGPLv3). It is fully functional on its own.

For teams that run ClickHouse® at scale, CHOps also offers a **Pro edition** that adds capabilities beyond single-cluster administration:

- **Audit logging**: a tamper-evident record of every DDL, DML, and login event, searchable and exportable, with retention policies for compliance reporting.
- **Scheduled email reports**: dashboard snapshots and alert digests delivered on a schedule, with customizable templates.
- **Extended alerting**: anomaly detection, alert dependencies, and richer routing on top of the threshold rules in the community edition.
- **Multi-cluster fleet management**: manage many ClickHouse® clusters from one place through lightweight sidecar agents, with rolling-restart orchestration, cross-cluster schema synchronization, and centralized configuration management.

Learn more at [ch-ops.io](https://ch-ops.io).
